Justice Barrett joined the liberal wing.
- **Campaign-finance limits struck down:** The Court [ruled 6-3](https://www.npr.org/2026/06/30/nx-s1-5827039/supreme-court-campaign-finance) that the 1974 contribution caps are unconstitutional, potentially reshaping political money.
- **Trans-athlete bans upheld:** The Court sided with West Virginia and Idaho laws barring transgender women from women's sports.
- **New student-loan rules take effect:** Provisions of the 2025 "One Big Beautiful Bill" reset borrowing limits and repayment plans, affecting roughly 43 million borrowers.
